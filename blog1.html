减肥不是减重，是减脂

Weight Loss is Fat Loss, Not Just Weight

February 9, 2026 Source: WeChat Video
Volume Matters

Same weight, different volume. Fat takes up 3x more space!

减肥它不是减重，肥它就是油，是脂肪。

脂肪很轻，不压秤，但是它体积非常大，占地方。体重不变了，不代表你没有瘦，所以不要对体重太过执着。

关键还是得看身体的维度和体质率的变化。如果你的维度变小了，说明你瘦了。

“我的体重真的没有明显下降，但是我的裤子肥了，我的背薄了，别人看见我都说瘦了。” — 那才是真的瘦了。

内行人看维度，外行人看斤数

平衡期： 它的基数是不会减的，甚至还有可能往上涨。但是你的体积缩小了，说明你是真的瘦了，皮肤收紧了。

Weight loss is not about the scale; "Fat" is essentially oil and lipids.

Fat is light and doesn't weigh much, but it has a massive volume. If your weight stays the same, it doesn't mean you haven't gotten thinner. Stop obsessing over the scale.

The key is your body measurements and fat percentage. If your dimensions are shrinking, you are winning.

"My weight hasn't dropped, but my pants are loose, my back is thinner, and everyone says I look slim." — That is true success.

Experts watch inches; Amateurs watch pounds.

Plateau Phase: Your weight might stagnate or even rise slightly. But if your volume is shrinking, your body is tightening and fat is melting away.
