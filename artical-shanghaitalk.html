超越体重秤：创办人 Dr. Sttanny 参与商海名人访谈，重塑现代体态美

Beyond the Scale: Dr. Sttanny Redefines Modern Beauty on Shanghai Talk

By Slim Paris Editorial | Feb 2026 | 5 Min Read
观看访谈 | Watch Interview

“这不仅仅是为了比别人更轻，而是关于在你自己独特的曲线中找到自信。”

最近，我们的创办人 Dr. Sttanny 受邀参与了著名的 《商海名人访》(Shanghai Talk)。在这次深度访谈中，她分享了 Slim Paris 品牌背后的初衷 —— 这是一段跨越 20 年，关于个人韧性与专业精神的旅程。

源于亲身经历的哲学

从 20 年前的一场车祸中康复，到亲身经历产后的身体变化，Dr. Sttanny 对美的理解是非常感性的。她意识到，“减肥”行业往往带来的焦虑多于解决办法。因此，Slim Paris 应运而生，旨在通过科学的方法，在健康与体态塑造之间建立桥梁。

“在我的为人母的过程中，我看到很多女性都在忍受‘身材焦虑’。她们不吃东西，失去了快乐。我想打造一个品牌，帮助女性找回她们的 S 曲线，同时不丢失她们的幸福感。”

曲线美 vs. 减重

访谈中的一个重要观点是“减重”与“塑形”的区别。Dr. Sttanny 解释说，Slim Paris 更注重女性的“气场” —— 她的仪态、纤薄的背部以及颈部的线条感。这些是体重秤无法衡量的，但却是世界能够感受到的美。

"It isn't about being lighter than others; it is about the confidence found in your own unique curves."

Recently, our founder, Dr. Sttanny, was featured on the prestigious Shanghai Talk (商海名人访). In a deep-dive conversation, she shared the heart behind Slim Paris—a journey that spans 20 years of personal resilience and professional mastery.

A Philosophy Forged in Experience

From surviving a life-altering car accident to navigating the physical changes of motherhood, Dr. Sttanny's approach to beauty is deeply personal. She realized that the "weight loss" industry often creates more anxiety than it solves. Slim Paris was born to bridge the gap between wellness and aesthetic sculpting.

"In my journey through motherhood, I saw so many women suffering from body anxiety. They stopped eating, they lost their joy. I wanted to build a brand that helps women regain their 'S-Line' without losing their happiness."

Curve Beauty vs. Weight Loss

One of the key takeaways from the interview was the distinction between slimming and sculpting. Dr. Sttanny explained that Slim Paris focuses on the "aura" of a woman—her posture, the thinness of her back, and the grace of her neckline. These are things a scale cannot measure, but the world can feel.
