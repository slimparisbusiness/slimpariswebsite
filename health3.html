Slim Paris 不仅关注表面紧致，更通过专业的塑形技术帮助您纠正体态。正如韩国艺人姜素拉和秀智的蜕变所示，矫正颈部线条能让上身更放松，下颌线更清晰立体。

居家小贴士：如何矫正“手机颈”

  • 定位：挺直坐姿，找到后脑勺下方的小骨头（枕骨）。
  • 动作：一手托住下巴，另一手扶住后脑。
  • 拉伸：轻轻向上提升后脑，同时收紧下巴，感受颈部的延伸。

Did you know that good posture can actually change your face shape and overall silhouette?

Many struggle with a blurry jawline, but this isn't always just about weight—it is often caused by "Tech-Neck." Constant slumping forward causes the neck and shoulders to collapse, making the jawline appear sagging and less defined.

At Slim Paris, we focus on more than just surface tightening. Our sculpting technology helps you realign your frame. As seen in the transformations of celebrities like Kang Sora and Suzy, correcting your posture makes the upper body appear more relaxed and the jawline significantly sharper.

Quick Tip: How to Correct Tech-Neck

  • Locate: Sit straight and find the small bony bump at the back of your head.
  • Position: Hold your chin with one hand and the back of your head with the other.
  • The Motion: Gently lift the back of your head upward while tucking your chin in.
