Slim Paris is Malaysia’s premier body sculpting sanctuary, blending timeless Parisian elegance with the harmony of Traditional Realignment and Modern, non-invasive technology.


Guided by our 'Figure First, Scale Second' philosophy, we go beyond simple weight loss to correct posture and sculpt graceful curves without pain.


More than a slimming center, we are a haven for empowerment, dedicated to helping every woman rediscover her confidence and embrace a lifestyle of beauty and freedom.

THE FOUNDER

Dr. Sttanny Phong

"Confidence should embark from a holistic view, beginning with your mindset and not solely from a number on a scale."

With this guiding principle, Dr. Sttanny embarked on a mission to redefine body sculpting, blending a combination of traditional and modern services.


Dr. Sttanny’s unwavering confidence stems from the transformative power of our services. We aim to provide you with results, that you would witness a remarkable change in the mirror—a testament to our commitment.
