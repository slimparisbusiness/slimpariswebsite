体重没减不代表没瘦

Weight Loss vs. Fat Loss

February 13, 2026 WeChat Video

维度胜过金数：右边是5磅松散的脂肪，左边是5磅紧致的肌肉。

Dimensions over decimals: 5lbs of bulky fat vs. 5lbs of lean, dense muscle.

打破体重的迷思：减肥的真相，在于脂肪的“体积”而非“重量”。

知道为什么吗？因为脂肪很轻，不压秤，但是它的体积非常大，占地方。脂肪的体积是肌肉的三倍。

体重不变不代表你没有瘦，所以不要对体重太过执着。关键还是得看身体维度和体质率的变化。如果你的维度变小了，说明你瘦了。

有的人体重不但没减，甚至还有可能往上涨。那是因肌肉率增加，体型反而更紧致。只要你的体型变好看了，说明你是真的瘦了。

“以前我只看体重秤，现在我只看镜子。虽然数字变动不大，但我的身型变得紧致，线条出来了，这才是我想要的成功。” — 重塑曲线比减轻重量更重要。

内行人看维度，外行人看体重

与其盯着数字看，不如相信你的镜子和衣服的宽松度！只要体型变美了，你就是成功的。

Breaking the weight myth: The truth about slimming lies in "volume," not just the number on the scale.

Do you know why? Because fat is very light, but its volume is massive. Fat takes up 3x more space than muscle.

A stable weight doesn't mean you aren't getting thinner. Don't obsess over the scale; focus on your body measurements and fat percentage. If your inches are dropping, you are losing fat.

Sometimes your weight might even increase as you gain lean muscle. If your silhouette is tighter and looks better, you are truly successful.

"I used to obsess over the scale; now I only look at the mirror. My weight is stable, but my silhouette is tighter and more defined. This is the change I actually wanted." — Sculpting curves is more vital than losing pounds.

Experts watch inches; Amateurs watch the scale.

Trust your mirror and the fit of your clothes more than the numbers on the scale! If your shape looks better, you've won.
