减肥不是减重

Weight Loss is Not Fat Loss

October 23, 2025 WeChat Video

同样的体重，不一样的体积。脂肪的体积是肌肉的3倍！

Same weight, different volume. Fat takes up 3x more space!

减肥它不是减重。肥它就是油，是脂肪！

脂肪它很轻，不压秤，但是它的体积非常大，占地方。所以体重不变，并不代表你没有瘦，不要对体重太过执着。

关键还是得看身体的维度和体质率的变化。如果你的维度变小了，就说明你瘦了。

在减肥的"平衡期"，你的基数可能不会减，甚至还有可能往上涨。但只要体积缩小了，皮肤收紧了，你就是真的瘦了。

"我的体重真的没有明显下降，但是我的裤子松了，我的背薄了，别人看见我都说我瘦了。" — 那才是真的瘦了。

内行人看维度，外行人看金数

只要你的体型变好看了，说明你是真的瘦了。与其盯着数字看，不如相信你的镜子和衣服的宽松度！

Weight loss is not fat loss. "Fat" is essentially oil and lipids!

Fat is incredibly light and doesn't weigh much on the scale, but its volume is massive—it takes up so much space! Even if the scale doesn't move, it doesn't mean you aren't transforming.

The real secret lies in your body measurements and body fat percentage. If your inches are dropping, you are losing fat.

During a "Plateau Period," your weight might stay the same or even go up! But if your volume is shrinking and your skin is tightening, you are truly getting thinner.

"My weight hasn't dropped significantly, but my clothes are loose, my back is thinner, and everyone says I look slim." — That is true success.

Experts watch inches; Amateurs watch the scale.

Stop obsessing over the numbers. If your silhouette looks better and your clothes fit differently, your fat is disappearing!
