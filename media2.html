放下对数字的追求：回归体态曲线美

Redefining The Ideal Body: Curve Beauty vs. Scale Numbers

February 2026 Source: CITYPlus Interview
Watch Full Interview
骨感并不是美的唯一标准。真正的美，是让身体回到它“最正确”的位置。

从车祸到自我重塑

Slim Paris 创办人 Dr. Sttanny 在访谈中透露，20年前的一场车祸曾让她面临严重的体态失衡。通过长期的体态研究，她发现美不应被“体重秤”绑架。真正的“气场”来自于均衡的曲线与优雅的体态。

数字的幻象

Dr. Sttanny 强调，维度（Inches）远比重量（Pounds）重要。通过体态管理，即便体重没有明显下降，视觉上也会看起来更高、更瘦、更有自信。

“倾听你的内心，而不是社会的标准。如果你在镜子里看到的自己是自信的，那就是美。”
Being bony is not the only standard of beauty. True beauty is about returning the body to its "rightful" place.

From Injury to Empowerment

Dr. Sttanny, founder of Slim Paris, shared how a major car accident 20 years ago left her with severe postural imbalances. This struggle taught her that beauty isn't found on a scale, but in the harmony of one's silhouette and personal "Aura".

The Scale Illusion

Dr. Sttanny advocates for "Body State Management"—focusing on measurements and body composition rather than total weight. By correcting posture, a woman can instantly look taller and radiate more confidence.

"Listen to your own heart, not societal standards. Beauty is an internal state of confidence that manifests as a powerful external aura."
